Headings and Paragraphs

  • Heading Tags: <h1> – <h6>

    <h1>Heading1</h1>
    <h2>Heading1</h2>
    <h3>Heading1</h3>

  • Paragraph Tags: <p>

    <p>This is my first paragraph</p>
    <p>This is my second paragraph</p>

  • Sections: <div> and <span>

    <div style="background: skyblue">
    This is a div
    </div>
